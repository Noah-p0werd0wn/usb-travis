
USAGE: kList [disconnect] [help]
             [interfaceguid <guid>] [instanceid <instanceid>]
                 
Commands:
  help (/?)  : Show this help text.
  disconnect : Include devices that are not currently connected.
                        
Switches:
  interfaceguid : Include only devices matching the specified
                  'DeviceInterfaceGUID'
  instanceid    : Include only devices matching the specified
                  'InstanceID'
          
Examples:

kList disconnect instanceid "USB\VID_04D8*" 
kList interfaceguid "{F676DCF6-FDFE-E0A9-FC12-8057DBE8E4B8}"
